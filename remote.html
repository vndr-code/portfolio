Custom Remote Control Systems – Signal Processing and Robotics

This project showcases a fully custom-built remote control system designed for wireless actuation of robotic components. The entire system, from PCB design to microcontroller programming, was developed from the ground up to ensure precise, interference-free signal transmission and execution.

1. Remote Control Transmitter

The remote control serves as the primary signal transmitter, encoding and wirelessly sending commands to the receiving end.

  • PCB & Circuit Design:
    • Designed and fabricated a custom PCB housing microcontrollers, signal modulation circuits, and power regulation components.
    • Integrated multiple push buttons, each mapped to distinct control signals.
  • Microcontroller Programming & Signal Transmission:
    • Programmed the microcontroller using Microchip Technology to encode signals.
    • Implemented reliable signal transmission ensuring distinct commands are sent without interference.
    • Verified signal integrity using LED indicators for different button presses.
  • Electrical Noise Reduction:
    • Incorporated capacitors and buffering techniques to stabilize the signal and mitigate electrical noise.
    • Ensured clean power delivery to prevent false triggering or interference with other electronics.

2. Wheelbarrow Robot – Receiver & Actuation

The receiving end of the system consists of a robot that decodes and executes movement commands based on the transmitted signals.

  • Receiver PCB & Microcontroller Integration:
    • Designed a dedicated PCB featuring a microcontroller and signal decoding circuitry.
    • Implemented electrical isolation techniques to ensure stable reception of transmitted signals.
  • Servo Motor Control & Movement Execution:
    • Used servo motors for movement, setting precise angles based on received signals.
    • Determined the appropriate signal pulse values to achieve accurate motor positioning.
    • Configured microcontroller logic to smoothly transition between servo positions, ensuring controlled motion.
  • Real-Time Signal Decoding & Execution:
    • The microcontroller continuously monitors incoming signals, mapping them to pre-set motor actions.
    • Verified consistent response across different environments, ensuring robust performance.

Future Enhancements & Applications

  • Expanding to multi-channel communication for simultaneous control of multiple devices.
  • Implementing alternative transmission methods (e.g., Bluetooth or RF-based signal encoding) for greater range and reliability.
  • Incorporating feedback loops for improved control precision and adaptive motion adjustments.
